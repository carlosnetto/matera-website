# Constitution — Matera Website Guidelines

Principles that apply to all content, design, and development decisions across both markets (/en and /br).

## Brand & Product Positioning

### Payment Imagery
- **Always use QR code and instant payment imagery** (person scanning QR code, Pix transfer, Zelle, FedNow) for payment visuals
- **Never use card payment imagery** (credit cards, debit cards, contactless card at POS, plastic) as the primary payment visual
- Matera's payment products are **QR Code payments and instant payments**, not card-based payments
- Card references in text are acceptable when describing supported payment rails (e.g., "push-to-card") or account types hosted by Digital Twin (e.g., "Credit Card accounts"), but cards should never be the hero image or primary visual

### Statistics (keep consistent across all pages)
- Pix transactions: **7.2 billion per year**
- QR Code payments: **3.6 billion per year**
- Digital accounts: **90 million+**
- Employees: **1,100+**
- Clients: **280+**

### Navigation Order (both markets)
- Solutions → News/Insights → Company → Contact

## Content Principles

- The live site (matera.com) always wins over source code when there's a conflict
- Content should match what's currently live on matera.com — check before assuming
- EN and BR are different markets with different products — don't translate, adapt
- All content images must have descriptive `alt` text (never `alt=""` for content images)
